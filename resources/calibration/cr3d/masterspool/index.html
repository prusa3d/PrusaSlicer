CR-3D Design Masterspool

For us as a filament manufacturer, it was important that our customers can process their samples properly, which is why the Masterspool was developed.

Design MasterSpool

  • less material
  • can be printed with larger nozzles
  • large outer diameter with 205 mm
  • angular design > prevents rolling away
  • big side cuts, easy to remove and add zip ties
  • side holes to fix filament ends
  • stiff side walls
